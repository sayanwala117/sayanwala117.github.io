About Me

I'm Jaspreet Jawanda — a passionate software developer and Computer Science & Business student at Wilfrid Laurier University, based in Ontario. I thrive on turning complex problems into clean, efficient solutions and love exploring new ways to push the boundaries of what's possible with code.
